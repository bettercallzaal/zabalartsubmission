Frequently Asked Questions

What types of submissions are accepted?

We accept all forms of creative work including artwork, music, remixes, videos, photography, memes, essays, creative code, Farcaster casts, and more. If it's inspired by the ZABAL ecosystem, we want to see it!

Do I retain rights to my work?

Yes! You retain all rights to your work. Credit always stays with the creator. We may showcase selected works on ZABAL.art and in community content, but ownership remains with you.

How long does the review process take?

Submissions are posted to our Discord immediately for community review. The ZABAL team reviews submissions on a rolling basis. Selected works will be featured on ZABAL.art and you'll be notified.

Can I submit multiple works?

Absolutely! We encourage multiple submissions. Share all your ZABAL-inspired creations with the community.

What file formats are supported?

We accept images (JPG, PNG, GIF), audio files (MP3, WAV), videos (MP4, MOV), PDFs, and zip files. If you have a different format, reach out to us on Discord.

Is there any compensation for submissions?

While we can't guarantee compensation, contributors may be highlighted in future ZABAL content and community posts. Selected creators may receive recognition and potential opportunities within the ZABAL ecosystem.

What Happens Next?

  • Your submission is automatically posted to the ZABAL Discord community for review
  • Selected works may be showcased on ZABAL.art
  • Contributors may be highlighted in future content or ZABAL community posts
  • Credit always stays with the creator
